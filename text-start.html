Instant Mac and Cheese recipe

This recipe tells you how to make instant mac and cheese in a instant pot.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • salt
  • heavy cream
  • cheddar cheese
  • Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Place the pasta, water, and salt into your Instant Pot.
  2. Lock the lid and pressure cook for three to five minutes.
  3. Quick release the steam to prevent the macaroni from overcooking.
  4. Lastly, open the lid and stir in the cheeses and heavy cream.
